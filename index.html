Heather Haskins is a senior journalism major at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln. She has written for the Daily Nebraskan, IANR News Service and NewsNetNebraska. She is a service chair for SEECA and is a club member of United by Yarn. She likes to read, knit, spin yarn and care for her pet hedgehog.
