Welcome to my website.

Heather Haskins is a graduate of the College of Journalism and Mass Communications at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln. She has written for the Daily Nebraskan, IANR News Service and NewsNetNebraska.

She is looking for a communications job that involves writing, meeting new people and traveling.

During college, she was a service chair for the club "Students for Education in Exotic and Companion Animals" and was a member of United by Yarn, the college knitting club. In her spare time, Heather likes to read, play piano, knit and care for her bearded dragon, Drogon and her pet hedgehog, Westin.
